# AI Speaker Confidence Coach — Product Requirements

## Core Outcome
Help the user become a top-tier speaker by improving:
- confidence
- articulation
- clarity
- public speaking
- executive presence
- interview performance
- read-aloud fluency
- listening and response quality
- impromptu speaking

## Training Pillars
1. Confidence Builder
2. Public Speaking Trainer
3. Articulation & Clarity Trainer
4. Reading Aloud & Verbal Fluency Trainer
5. Impromptu Speaking Trainer
6. Executive Communication Trainer
7. Listening & Response Trainer

## MVP Features
- user onboarding
- goal selection
- daily training session generator
- voice recording
- transcript generation
- written AI feedback
- read-aloud module
- articulation module
- impromptu prompt module
- scoring dashboard
- session history
- weekly review

## Post-MVP Features
- real-time voice coach
- text-to-speech coach
- camera/body-language analysis
- slide presentation rehearsal
- live Q&A simulation
- multi-user speaking club

## Daily Session Flow
Default 20-minute session:
1. check-in — 1 min
2. breathing/reset — 2 min
3. articulation warmup — 3 min
4. read-aloud drill — 4 min
5. speaking rep — 5 min
6. AI feedback — 2 min
7. improved retry — 2 min
8. reflection and score — 1 min

## Scoring Categories
- Confidence Score
- Clarity Score
- Articulation Score
- Reading Fluency Score
- Concision Score
- Executive Presence Score
- Listening Score
- Impromptu Score
- Presentation Score

## Levels
1. Stabilise
2. Basic Clarity
3. Controlled Delivery
4. Strong Articulation
5. Confident Reading
6. Impromptu Control
7. Executive Presence
8. Persuasive Speaker
9. High-Stakes Performer
10. Elite Communicator

## Tone
Direct, practical, performance-focused, executive-level.
